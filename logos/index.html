Usage Guidelines

  • Clear space: Maintain padding equal to the height of the "o" around the logo
  • Minimum size: 80px width for digital, 20mm for print
  • Blue variant is primary; use orange for accent or dark backgrounds
  • Never stretch, rotate, or alter the logo proportions
  • Use wordmark for headers/navigation; app icon for compact spaces
